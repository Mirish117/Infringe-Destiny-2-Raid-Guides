01
Chapel of Dreams
Encounter 1

Alternate between the real world and Calus's dream world to ring a set of bells in the correct order.

Mechanic: Dream Realm

Entering a gong sends certain players into the Dream Realm where the dungeon's key mechanic takes place. Dream Realm players must shoot specific bell totems while Real World players defend.

Dream Realm (1-2)
Enter the dream — shoot the glowing bell on the wall matching your symbol
Real World (1-2)
Hold off Cabal adds — prevent the wipe mechanic from triggering
  1. One player enters the dream by standing under the portal
  2. Read the bell symbol displayed on the dream wall
  3. Shoot the matching bell — this rings it in the real world
  4. Real world players kill the Unstoppable Champion that spawns when the bell rings
  5. Repeat for all three bells — then DPS the midboss
Tips & Notes
  • Dream Realm players cannot be damaged in the dream — focus purely on the bells
  • Real World players: Unstoppable Champions must be stunned before they reach the bell — Unstoppable mods mandatory
  • Bell sequences must be completed within the round timer or it resets
02
Vault of Pride
Encounter 2

A larger version of the Chapel mechanic with more complex bell sequences and heavier Cabal defenders.

  1. Two players enter the dream simultaneously
  2. Each reads and shoots their assigned bell
  3. Real world team kills the double Champion spawn
  4. Three rounds of bells complete the encounter
Tips & Notes
  • Assign specific bells to each dream player before entering — overlap wastes the window
  • The vault layout mirrors the chapel but doubled in scale — orient yourself immediately upon entering
  • Exotic Gjallarhorn or equivalent burst DPS eliminates Champions quickly in the real world
03
Caiatl
Final Boss

Caiatl, daughter of Calus. The dungeon's final boss using the bell mechanic combined with direct damage phases.

Recommended Loadout
  • Best-in-slot DPS — rockets or linears
  • Well of Radiance or Tether
  • Unstoppable Champion mods mandatory
  1. Dream players ring the required bells to open Caiatl's damage window
  2. Real world players suppress Champions
  3. Caiatl vulnerable — all three players dump DPS into her weak point
  4. Repeat until defeated
Tips & Notes
  • Caiatl's shield is linked to specific bell counts — more bells = longer damage window
  • Her enrage attack at low health is her most dangerous — save burst supers for the final phase
  • In Challenge Mode: each bell must be rung by a different player each round
