01
Engram Cave
Opening

Navigate a Fallen cave system while managing the Burden of Riches debuff. Picking up engrams from dead Vandals charges the debuff — deposit them in the hopper before the stack kills you.

Tips & Notes
  • Carrying more than a certain engram count rapidly stacks the debuff — deposit frequently
  • Hoppers are marked on the minimap — familiarize yourself with their locations before diving in
  • The secret loot chest in this section requires a specific path through the waterfall area
02
Sparrow Mine Section
Encounter 1

Ride Sparrows through an active mine field while managing Scorch Cannon pickups to destroy door-blocking Shielded Servitors.

  1. Pick up the Scorch Cannon from fallen Fallen enemies
  2. Locate the Shielded Servitor blocking the next section
  3. Fire the Cannon to strip the shield — teammates finish the Servitor
  4. Progress to the next section
Tips & Notes
  • The Sparrow mine fields are precise — memorize safe corridors on your second run
  • Multiple Scorch Cannons drop simultaneously — assign targets to avoid wasting shots
  • Fallen Captains in this section have Immunity Shields — Scorch Cannon strips them
03
Avarokk, the Covetous
Final Boss

Avarokk is a powerful Fallen Vandal. The Scorch Cannon mechanic returns combined with a two-phase DPS cycle.

Recommended Loadout
  • Rockets with autoloading for sustained DPS
  • Scorch Cannon for the mechanic phase
  • Suppressor grenades for Avarokk's retreat
  1. Kill adds to generate Scorch Cannon drops
  2. Use the Cannon to destroy Avarokk's shield (multiple shots required)
  3. Shield down — all three players damage Avarokk's weak point
  4. Avarokk retreats behind a gate — repeat Cannon cycle to re-open him
  5. Repeat until defeated
Tips & Notes
  • Stagger Scorch Cannon shots slightly — back-to-back shots can accidentally count as one
  • Avarokk spawns Dregs that suicide-rush the fireteam during the mechanic phase — clear them fast
  • In the final phase, Avarokk's shield recharges faster — increase Cannon production by killing more adds
