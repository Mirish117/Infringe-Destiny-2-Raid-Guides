Symbols to Memorize

Last Wish relies heavily on symbol callouts. Your team does not have to use one exact naming system, but everyone should agree on the same names before starting the raid.

Symbol callout reference
Team Tip

Keep one player responsible for confirming symbol calls. Most wipes in this raid happen from mixed callouts, rushed calls, or two players using different names for the same symbol.

1

Kalli, the Corrupted

Kalli teaches the raid's core symbol language. Players match symbols, cleanse plates, kill Taken Knights, damage Kalli, then hide inside safe doors to survive her wipe attack.

Kalli encounter layout
Kalli encounter instructions

Mechanics

  1. Read the symbols in the center of the arena.
  2. Each player goes to a plate matching one of those symbols.
  3. Stand on the clear section of the plate and avoid the Taken blights.
  4. Kill the Taken Knight that spawns after cleansing the plate.
  5. Group in the center for damage when Kalli becomes vulnerable.
  6. When Kalli prepares her weapon, enter one open door. Only one player should use each door.
Challenge Mode

Cleanse all nine plates before the damage phase begins.

2

Shuro Chi, the Corrupted

Shuro Chi is a timed encounter with add clear, crystal shield breaks, damage windows, and puzzle rooms. The team must move quickly because Shuro Chi's Song acts as the wipe timer.

Shuro Chi encounter layout
Shuro Chi puzzle room guide

Roles

Shield Breakers

Three players pick up crystals and connect beams to break Shuro Chi's shield.

Puzzle Players

Four players handle the puzzle room plates and complete the missing-image pattern.

Mechanics

  1. Clear enemies quickly and move forward as a team.
  2. Shield breakers pick up crystals and form a triangle beam to remove Shuro Chi's shield.
  3. Damage Shuro Chi until the health gate is reached.
  4. Enter the puzzle room and complete the wall pattern using the floor plates.
  5. Repeat the process until Shuro Chi is defeated.

Morgeth, the Spirekeeper

Morgeth is about collecting Taken Strength, freeing trapped players, and starting damage before Morgeth reaches full strength.

Morgeth encounter layout. This view is cropped to the top portion so the map stays readable.

Morgeth Mechanics Written Out

  1. Start the encounter by picking up the first Taken Strength orb.
  2. Taken Strength appears around the arena. Assigned players collect it and call out their stack count.
  3. A player can become trapped. Call out immediately when trapped.
  4. Kill the Eye of Riven and pick up the Taken Essence.
  5. Use the Taken Essence grenade ability to cleanse/free the trapped player.
  6. Do not let Morgeth reach 100% strength. Start damage before the wipe occurs.
  7. During damage, group at the agreed damage spot and use heavy weapons, supers, and debuffs.
  8. If Morgeth survives, repeat the Strength collection and cleanse cycle.
Simple Callouts

Use clear location names such as left front, left back, right front, right back, and middle. The cleaner your callouts are, the easier this encounter becomes.

Strength Collectors

Pick up Taken Strength and constantly call your stack count.

Cleanser

Use Taken Essence to free trapped players before the team loses control.

4

The Vault

The Vault is a symbol-reading encounter. Players read plates, identify matching symbols, determine Penumbra or Antumbra, then runners cleanse the correct plates with Taken Essence.

Vault encounter layout
Vault encounter diagram
Vault encounter instructions

Mechanics

  1. Three readers stand on the plates and read their symbols.
  2. Readers identify which symbol appears on another player's plate.
  3. The team determines whether each plate needs Penumbra or Antumbra.
  4. The runner grabs Taken Essence and cleanses the correct plate.
  5. Kill Knights before they reach the plates.
  6. Repeat until the Vault is complete.

Riven of a Thousand Voices

Riven is the final boss encounter. It uses split teams, symbol reading, elevator rooms, eye callouts, and coordinated damage.

Riven encounter layout
Riven eye callout chart
Riven floor pattern
Riven floor pattern 2

Mechanics

  1. Split into two teams of three.
  2. Call out Riven's real eyes using the eye chart.
  3. Shoot only the correct eyes. Shooting the wrong eye can wipe the team.
  4. Handle symbol/floor pattern mechanics when assigned.
  5. Regroup for damage and repeat until Riven is defeated.
6

Riven's Heart

After Riven is defeated, the fireteam carries Riven's Heart through Queenswalk. The heart pulls players inside one by one, so carrier rotation and clean pathing are the key mechanics.

Riven final stand encounter
Final stand instructions
Final dunk ending

Mechanics

  1. Pick up Riven's Heart and move as a group.
  2. The carrier calls out the timer clearly.
  3. When the carrier gets pulled inside, the next player picks up the heart.
  4. Players inside collect Taken Strength to extend the outside timer.
  5. Continue rotating carriers until the final dunk.
