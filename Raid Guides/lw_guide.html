01
Kalli, the Corrupted
Encounter 1

Stand on nine plates in a specific wish-symbol order. Six of the nine plates must be activated correctly per round while killing adds.

Readers (2)
Memorize the 4-symbol sequence displayed at the start — usually called via screenshot
Plate Activators
Each player takes assigned plates — activate in the correct order
Tips & Notes
  • The sequence is determined by the wish you're running — agree beforehand
  • If the wrong plate is activated the encounter resets without a wipe — but correct plates must all be activated in the same round
  • Kalli executes a wipe mechanic at the end of each round — deal damage to interrupt it
02
Shuro Chi, the Corrupted
Encounter 2

A gauntlet-and-DPS encounter. The team must solve a symbol puzzle each phase while Shuro Chi sings toward a wipe countdown.

Mechanic: Symbol Matching

Three circles appear on the wall — each displaying a symbol. Three players each look at a different book around the arena to identify which symbol matches their book. Call out 'match' or 'no match' and players rotate positions to align all three symbols.

  1. Phase begins — read your book symbol and call if it matches your circle
  2. Players rotate clockwise or counterclockwise until all three circles match the books
  3. All three matched = damage window opens
  4. Shuro Chi takes damage — repeat for next platform
Tips & Notes
  • Rotating the wrong way wastes precious time — agree on rotation direction beforehand
  • Shuro Chi's wipe mechanic targets the player she's facing — stay mobile
  • The Wish-Ender bow can reveal secrets in this area if you need to hunt for hidden lore
03
Morgeth, the Spirekeeper
Encounter 3

Collect Taken Strength motes while keeping them balanced between two players. If one player reaches 5 stacks they become Taken.

Mote Carriers (2)
Collect motes — keep stacks near equal, never let one person hit 5
Add Clear (4)
Kill Taken enemies to generate motes and suppress Ogres
  1. Morgeth spawns — start collecting motes from Taken enemies
  2. Two designated players collect — call your stack count constantly
  3. At 2 mote stacks each, use the Burden of the Taken ability to stagger Morgeth
  4. Repeat until Morgeth reaches 5 stacks — final DPS window
Tips & Notes
  • If one carrier hits 5 stacks they get wiped — split pickup duties evenly
  • The Burden of the Taken ability wipe is instant if not activated in time
  • Two-phasing Morgeth is easily achievable with good mote collection
04
Vault
Encounter 4

A timed vault traversal. Players must activate plates in a specific order within a time limit while navigating multiple floors of the vault.

  1. Split into three pairs — one pair per vault level
  2. Each pair activates their level's plates in the sequence called by the reader
  3. All plates active simultaneously opens the vault door
Tips & Notes
  • The sequence changes each attempt — re-read every time
  • Failed plate presses force a re-clear of the entire level
  • Move fast — the timer does not forgive standing still
05
Riven of a Thousand Voices
Final Boss

The most complex encounter in Last Wish. Riven spans two separate rooms — her heart must be destroyed in a specific sequence.

Phase 1 — Eyes
  1. Two teams of three split between Room A and Room B
  2. Kill adds to spawn Taken Strength motes
  3. Collect motes to receive Taken Essence
  4. Use Taken Essence to shoot Riven's eyes — she has multiple fake eyes, only the real one glows bright
  5. Shoot the correct eye to stagger Riven
Phase 2 — Heart
  1. After Riven falls, the two teams must find each other in the correct rooms
  2. The team with Riven's heart must traverse to the other team's location
  3. Carry the heart — it periodically blinds the carrier
  4. Dunk the heart into the receptacle before time expires
Tips & Notes
  • Calling the correct eye is critical — communicate 'top-left, second from right' style coordinates
  • The heart carrier cannot shoot — protect them vigorously
  • In Challenge Mode: both teams must carry the heart equally — swap the carrier each dunk
  • Queenswalk (the final encounter) begins immediately after Riven — prepare
06
Queenswalk
Final Encounter

Carry Riven's heart through the Dreaming City to the Wishing Well and feed it to Sjur Eido.

  1. Pick up the heart — it explodes periodically, doing massive damage
  2. Navigate the corridors back toward the entrance
  3. Rotate heart carrier between three designated players to distribute the damage
  4. Dunk the heart at the Wishing Well to complete the raid
Tips & Notes
  • Stagger heart rotations so the carrier always has near-full health
  • Taken Phalanxes along the route can one-shot — play cautiously
  • This is not a combat encounter — focus purely on safe traversal
