Raid Overview
Start Here

Last Wish is a full raid with multiple symbol-heavy encounters. The team needs strong communication, clear callouts, and assigned roles before starting each fight.

Symbol callout reference
Recommended Preparation
  • Assign symbol readers before encounters that require callouts.
  • Keep damage weapons ready for bosses and add-clear weapons ready for Taken waves.
  • Make sure every player understands the wipe mechanic before starting each encounter.

02
Kalli, the Corrupted
Encounter 1

Kalli is the first main encounter. Players activate correct plates, survive Taken waves, then group for damage while avoiding the wipe mechanic.

Kalli encounter layout
Kalli encounter instructions
Readers
Call out the symbols and make sure players know which plates to take.
Plate Players
Stand on assigned plates, clear Taken Knights, and rotate safely.
Damage Team
Group up for DPS when the safe damage window opens.
Tips & Notes
  • Do not stand on the wrong plate.
  • Kill the Taken Knight that spawns on your plate.
  • When doors open under Kalli, hide inside the correct safe doors to survive.
03
Shuro Chi, the Corrupted
Encounter 2

Shuro Chi is a fast encounter with add clear, damage phases, and puzzle rooms. Your team needs to move quickly because the wipe timer is constantly counting down.

Shuro Chi encounter layout
Shuro Chi puzzle rooms
Mechanic: Puzzle Rooms
  1. Clear enemies quickly and push forward as a group.
  2. Damage Shuro Chi when the phase opens.
  3. Move into the puzzle room when the team reaches it.
  4. Players stand on correct plates to complete the image puzzle.
  5. Repeat until Shuro Chi is defeated.
Tips & Notes
  • Assign puzzle players before starting.
  • Do not step on the same puzzle plate twice unless your team’s strategy requires it.
  • Keep moving because the wipe timer does not stop.
04
Morgeth, the Spirekeeper
Encounter 3

Morgeth focuses on collecting Taken Strength and managing trapped players. Communication is important because players need to track who has strength and who needs to be cleansed.

Morgeth encounter top layout

Morgeth Mechanics — Readable Text Version

Main goal: Collect Taken Strength around the arena, free trapped players, and start damage before Morgeth reaches full strength.

Taken Strength: Players pick up Taken Strength orbs and call out their stack count. Do not let one player accidentally take too many stacks.

Trapped player: Morgeth can trap a teammate. A player with Taken Essence must cleanse them quickly before the team loses control of the encounter.

Damage phase: Once the team has managed the required Taken Strength and everyone is safe, group up and damage Morgeth.

Strength Collectors
Pick up Taken Strength and call out your stacks.
Cleanse Player
Use the Taken Essence to free trapped teammates.
Add Clear
Control enemies so collectors can safely move around the arena.
  1. Start the encounter and begin clearing enemies.
  2. Collect Taken Strength while calling out stack counts.
  3. Free trapped players using Taken Essence.
  4. Begin damage when the team is ready.
  5. Repeat if Morgeth survives the damage phase.
Tips & Notes
  • Do not let one player accidentally take too many stacks.
  • Call out immediately when trapped.
  • Save heavy ammo and supers for the main damage phase.
05
The Vault
Encounter 4

The Vault is one of the most communication-heavy parts of Last Wish. Players read symbols, call out Penumbra or Antumbra, and cleanse the correct plates.

Vault encounter overview
Vault encounter diagram
Vault encounter instructions
Plate Readers
Read the center symbol and call the matching left or right symbol.
Runners
Pick up the Taken Essence and cleanse the correct plate.
Add Clear
Keep the rooms controlled so readers and runners can focus.
  1. Three players stand on the plates and read their symbols.
  2. Call out which symbol is duplicated between plates.
  3. Determine which plates need Penumbra or Antumbra.
  4. Runner picks up Taken Essence and cleanses the correct plate.
  5. Repeat until the vault is complete.
Tips & Notes
  • Use consistent callouts: left, middle, right.
  • Do not cleanse a plate unless the team confirms it.
  • Keep Knights away from the plates.
06
Riven of a Thousand Voices
Final Boss

Riven is the final boss encounter before Queenswalk. The encounter uses split teams, symbol reading, eye callouts, floor patterns, and coordinated damage.

Riven encounter overview
Riven eye pattern callouts
Riven floor pattern
Riven floor pattern 2
Mechanic: Eye Callouts
  1. Split into two teams.
  2. Identify which eyes need to be shot.
  3. Call eye positions clearly using the eye chart.
  4. Shoot only the correct eyes.
  5. Group for damage when the encounter reaches the damage phase.
Tips & Notes
  • Bad eye callouts can wipe the team quickly.
  • Use the same eye names every run.
  • Make sure every player knows whether your team is doing normal mechanics or a cheese strategy.
07
Riven Final Stand & Queenswalk
Final Encounter

After Riven is defeated, the team must complete Queenswalk by carrying Riven’s Heart. Players rotate heart carrier duties while the rest of the team clears the path and protects the run.

Riven final stand encounter
Final dunk ending
Final stand instructions
  1. Pick up Riven’s Heart.
  2. Move together through the path.
  3. Rotate carriers when the heart pulls players inside.
  4. Clear enemies quickly and keep the path safe.
  5. Dunk the heart to complete the raid.
Tips & Notes
  • Call out when your timer is low.
  • Stay together so no one gets left behind.
  • Inside players should collect Taken Strength to extend the carrier’s timer.
