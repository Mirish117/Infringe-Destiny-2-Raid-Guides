Open the Gate

The opening encounter requires the team to hold three sync plates outside the Vault of Glass entrance until the spire forms.

Open Gate encounter
Gate opening guide

Roles

Left Plate

Hold left plate and clear Vex waves.

Middle Plate

Hold center plate and help whichever side is pressured.

Right Plate

Hold right plate and prevent Vex from capturing it.

Mechanics

  1. Split into three teams of two.
  2. Stand on all three plates until the spire begins forming.
  3. Kill Praetorians and Vex before they retake the plates.
  4. Keep all plates controlled until the Vault entrance opens.
2

Confluxes

Confluxes is a defense encounter. The team prevents Vex sacrifices at active confluxes while managing Fanatics and cleansing.

Defend the Confluxes
Conflux guide

Mechanics

  1. Assign players to left, middle, and right defense positions.
  2. Kill Vex before they sacrifice at the active confluxes.
  3. Watch for Fanatics and avoid standing in their pools.
  4. Cleanse at the center if you become Marked for Negation.
  5. Continue defending until all waves are complete.
Team Tip

Do not chase enemies away from your lane. The encounter is easier when each player protects their assigned side.

3

Oracles

Oracles appear in a sequence and must be destroyed in that exact order. Assign callout locations before starting.

Oracle callouts
Oracles guide

Mechanics

  1. Listen for Oracle spawn order.
  2. Call each Oracle location clearly.
  3. Destroy Oracles in the exact order they appear.
  4. If the team becomes marked, cleanse before the wipe.
  5. Repeat through all Oracle waves.
4

Templar

The Templar combines Oracle management, relic cleansing, teleport blocking, and boss damage.

Templar encounter
Templar guide

Roles

Relic Holder

Picks up the relic, cleanses teammates, and breaks Templar's shield.

Oracle Team

Kills Oracles in order and prevents marked wipes.

Teleport Blocker

Blocks Templar's teleport by standing in the teleport ring.

Mechanics

  1. Relic holder picks up the relic and charges the relic super.
  2. Destroy Oracles in the correct order.
  3. Relic holder uses super to break Templar's shield.
  4. Damage Templar while blocking teleports if your team is doing the challenge/fast strategy.
  5. Cleanse players if they become Marked for Negation.
5

Gorgon's Labyrinth

The Labyrinth is a stealth traversal section. The team must move through the maze without being seen by Gorgons.

Gorgon's Labyrinth
Labyrinth guide

Mechanics

  1. Move as a group and follow the route caller.
  2. Avoid Gorgon line of sight.
  3. Do not sprint or jump into patrol paths without a callout.
  4. Regroup at the exit before moving forward.
6

Gatekeepers

Gatekeepers uses relic rotation, portals, Praetorian control, and plate defense. Players must send the relic between Venus and Mars while keeping the main arena stable.

Gatekeepers encounter
Gatekeeper guide

Roles

Relic Runner

Carries the relic into portals and kills shielded Praetorians.

Portal Teams

Defend Venus and Mars plates and call when relic support is needed.

Mid Clear

Kills Gatekeepers, Wyverns, and enemies in the center room.

Mechanics

  1. Open Venus and Mars portals by holding plates.
  2. Relic runner enters the side that has a shielded Praetorian.
  3. Kill Praetorians and keep confluxes safe inside portals.
  4. Rotate relic between sides as needed.
  5. After portal phases, defend the center conflux until the encounter ends.
7

Atheon, Time's Conflux

Atheon teleports three players into Venus or Mars. The teleported team handles relic and Oracle order, while the outside team opens the correct portal and prepares for damage.

Atheon encounter
Atheon guide
Atheon oracle callouts

Roles

Teleported Team

Handles relic, Oracles, cleansing, and return callouts.

Outside Team

Opens the correct portal and keeps the main arena clear.

Oracle Caller

Calls Oracle order using the agreed callout chart.

Mechanics

  1. Three players are teleported into Venus or Mars.
  2. Outside team calls which portal to open.
  3. Inside team picks up relic and kills Oracles in the correct order.
  4. Relic holder cleanses blindness.
  5. Return to the main arena and group for damage.
  6. Watch for Detainment and call it out immediately.
  7. Repeat until final stand and finish Atheon.
Detainment Rule

If you are detained during damage, call it immediately and move away from the group. Teammates must break the detain bubble quickly.
