01
Basilica
Traversal

The opening section of King's Fall requires the fireteam to cross a collapsing platform section by staying in the light of a mobile lamp.

  1. Three players must grab the three Relic Crystals scattered around the starting area
  2. The team moves together — the lamp holder must stay ahead of the group
  3. Players too far from the lamp get wiped by the Darkness
Tips & Notes
  • Never let the lamp get more than two platforms ahead — call 'hold' if you need a second
  • The lamp transitions between players — communicate who picks it up each time
  • Watch for the hidden chest in the platform area — it's below the main path on the right side
02
Totems
Encounter 1

Stand on two totem platforms simultaneously while managing a wipe mechanic caused by the Touch of Malice debuff.

Totem Holders (2)
Stand on the two outer totems — alternating between each other to shed the debuff before it stacks too high
Knightslayers (2)
Kill the Knight that spawns center-arena to prevent a wipe
Runner (1)
Collects the Aura of Immortality from the Knight kill and carries it to the pool
Floater (1)
Rotates help between totem holders and runners as needed
  1. Two players stand on the totems simultaneously
  2. Knightslayers kill the Knight in the center
  3. The player who lands the final blow gets the Aura — carry it to the pool
  4. Rotate totem holders before Touch of Malice reaches max stacks
Tips & Notes
  • Assign Left Totem and Right Totem roles before the encounter starts
  • The Aura runner should immediately start moving toward the pool the moment the Knight dies
  • If both totem holders die simultaneously the encounter wipes — never leave both untended
03
Warpriest
Encounter 2

The Warpriest must be killed during a brief damage window. The damage window is only unlocked by activating three brand-bearer plates in a specific order.

Mechanic: Glyph Order

Three glyphs on the wall correspond to three plates in the arena. Before the encounter, read the glyph order (top glyph = first plate). The same three Guardians must stand on the three plates in the correct order to open the damage window.

Glyph Reader
Calls out plate order based on the glyphs — Left, Middle, Right
Brand Bearer (3)
Stand on the plates in the correct order to generate the damage window
Add Clear (2)
Suppresses Hive while the brand bearers activate plates and during DPS
Tips & Notes
  • Read the glyphs before picking up any Brand — the order resets each attempt
  • The Brand Bearer role rotates each DPS window to distribute the debuff
  • Pop supers at the start of the damage window, not the end — you'll run out of time
04
Golgoroth
Encounter 3

Golgoroth's gaze must be captured by one player to expose his weak point while the rest of the team deals damage. The gaze rotates on a timer.

Mechanic: Gaze

One player captures Golgoroth's attention by shooting a specific orb on the ceiling. Golgoroth turns to face them. The fireteam has a short window to damage his stomach weak point while the gaze holder stays alive.

Gaze Holder
Shoots the ceiling orb, faces Golgoroth, kites around the arena
DPS Team
Focus fire on Golgoroth's exposed stomach
Ogre Suppressor
Kill the Ogres that spawn from the pits to prevent adds from overwhelming
  1. Gaze Holder shoots the ceiling orb to claim Golgoroth's attention
  2. Team pivots to damage the glowing stomach
  3. Before the gaze timer expires, the next Gaze Holder shoots the next ceiling orb
  4. Gaze transfers — old holder rejoins DPS
Tips & Notes
  • The new Gaze Holder MUST shoot their orb before the current timer expires or Golgoroth wipes the team
  • Assign gaze order 1-2-3 before starting
  • Orbs spawn in a fixed pattern around the ceiling — learn the rotation
05
Daughters
Encounter 4

Two bosses — Ir Anûk and Ir Halak — stand on platforms at opposite ends of the arena. Players must alternate between killing adds and depositing Corrupted Light bombs.

Mechanic: Corrupted Light

Killing Tombship adds causes them to drop Corrupted Light bombs. These must be collected and detonated under the active Daughter before her shield resets.

Bomb Runner (2)
Pick up Corrupted Light and deposit it under the active Daughter
Add Clear (4)
Kill Tombships to generate bombs and suppress the Wizard reinforcements
Tips & Notes
  • Both Daughters become active simultaneously in the final phase — split bomb runners accordingly
  • Do not detonate Corrupted Light too early — wait for the Daughter to lower her shield by stepping on the plate
  • Supers are best saved for the final phase when both are active
06
Oryx
Final Boss

The Taken King himself. A multi-phase encounter requiring Corrupted Light, glyph plates, and a massive slam to stagger Oryx out of his shield.

Recommended Loadout
  • Best DPS loadout possible — Anarchy, Rocket Launchers, or Cataclysmic
  • Well of Radiance for all DPS phases
  • Ward of Dawn optional for soaking Oryx's chest beam
Phase 1 — Relic
  1. Kill four Ogres at the corners of the arena to spawn four Corrupted Light bombs
  2. Assign four players to each bomb
  3. All four detonate simultaneously — this stuns Oryx and exposes his chest
  4. Two players grab the Knights' Relics to bait Oryx's slam attack
  5. After the slam, Oryx is stunned — DPS his chest
Phase 2 — Darkness Realm

After enough stagger phases, Oryx retreats into the Darkness Realm. The fireteam is sucked in and must kill a massive Shade of Oryx by shooting the correctly glowing glyph plates.

Enrage

If Oryx is not defeated within a set number of cycles, he enrages and oneshots the fireteam. Push for a clean six-phase or better.

Tips & Notes
  • All four Corrupted Lights MUST detonate within a short window of each other — designate a countdown caller
  • The Relic holders must wait for Oryx's hands to glow before triggering the slam — too early and the stagger fails
  • In the Darkness Realm, only the correctly glowing plates damage Oryx's Shade — wrong plates do nothing
