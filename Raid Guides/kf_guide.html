Deepsight Chest Symbols

The King's Fall red-border chest requires your team to identify and shoot the correct deepsight symbols throughout the raid. Have one player track these symbols from the start.

Deepsight rune symbols
Deepsight symbol location pattern
Team Tip

Call out symbols as soon as they appear. Do not wait until the end of the raid to confirm them, because missed symbols can cost the red-border chest.

1

Court of Oryx

The opening Court of Oryx section is a relic dunk encounter. Players retrieve relics from both sides, escort the runners, open taken barriers, and dunk relics into the statues.

Court of Oryx encounter
Court of Oryx guide

Mechanics

  1. Split into left and right teams.
  2. Relic runners pick up the orbs from each side.
  3. Escort players shoot Taken barriers and clear enemies.
  4. Both relics must be dunked close together at the statue.
  5. Repeat until all statue deposits are complete.
  6. Push through the opened portal to enter Oryx's throne world.
Challenge / Triumph Note

Rotate dunkers so the same player does not repeatedly deposit relics before everyone else has had a turn.

2

The Crux & Portico

This section is mainly traversal. The fireteam crosses the tombship/crux area, times jumps, and can open the hidden chest using pressure plates.

The Crux traversal

Mechanics

  1. Move as a team through the tombship path.
  2. Time jumps carefully. Do not rush players who are still learning the ship pattern.
  3. For the hidden chest, split players across the pressure plates to open the route.
  4. Send the chest team through, then rotate so the remaining players can claim it.
3

Basilica / Totems

Basilica is the first major team-rotation encounter. Players hold the Annihilator Totems, steal the Brand, build Deathsinger's Power, then deposit that power in the center plate.

Basilica encounter
Totems guide

Roles

Brand Holder

Stands under the totem and kills enemies to build Deathsinger's Power.

Brand Stealer

Kills the wizard and Blightguard, grabs the Brand Claimer, then steals the Brand.

Depositor

Deposits Deathsinger's Power in the center plate and helps clear middle enemies.

Mechanics

  1. Split into left and right teams of three.
  2. Brand holders stand at their totems and kill enemies.
  3. Brand stealers kill their wizard and Blightguard.
  4. Stealers take the Brand Claimer and steal the Brand before the timer expires.
  5. The old holder rotates to the center and deposits Deathsinger's Power.
  6. Repeat the rotation until the center door opens.
Challenge Mode

The Grass is Always Greener: players cannot take the same Brand type twice in a row.

4

Warpriest

Warpriest is a plate-reading boss encounter. Players read the glowing sequence, step on plates in order, gain the Brand, extend the Brand with Knight kills, and use pillars to survive the Oculus wipe mechanic.

Warpriest encounter
Warpriest guide

Roles

Plate Readers

Stand left, middle, and right to read the plate order.

Brand Holder

Holds the damage aura so the team can damage Warpriest.

Knight Killers

Kill Brand Claimer Knights and refresh the Brand before time expires.

Mechanics

  1. Clear enemies until the glyph sequence begins.
  2. Read the plate order from the glowing plate/pillar sequence.
  3. Step on the plates in the correct order.
  4. Group for damage inside the Brand aura.
  5. Kill Brand Claimer Knights to extend the damage phase.
  6. Hide behind the correct pillar when Warpriest starts the Oculus wipe.

Golgoroth's Cellar

Golgoroth's Cellar is a maze/traversal section before the Golgoroth boss encounter. Use the guide image to keep the team moving in the correct direction.

Golgoroth's Cellar guide

Mechanics

  1. Move as a group through the cellar.
  2. Use the map/guide image to avoid getting separated.
  3. Clear enemies before pushing into the next section.
6

Golgoroth

Golgoroth requires gaze management. One player holds Golgoroth's Gaze while the damage team stands in pools of reclaimed light and damages his exposed weak point.

Golgoroth encounter
Golgoroth guide

Roles

Gaze Holders

Take Golgoroth's Gaze by shooting his back and manage the timer.

DPS Team

Shoot the ceiling orb, stand in the damage pool, and damage Golgoroth's stomach.

Mechanics

  1. Clear enemies until the center orb appears.
  2. Drop a pool by shooting the orb above the arena.
  3. One gaze holder shoots Golgoroth's back to take gaze.
  4. DPS team stands in the pool and shoots Golgoroth's stomach.
  5. Second gaze holder takes gaze before the timer runs out.
  6. Repeat pool and gaze rotation until damage ends.
Important

Call gaze timer clearly. A missed gaze transfer can end the damage phase or wipe the team.

7

Transept

The Transept is another traversal section with wall launchers and narrow platforms. Slow movement and clean callouts are better than rushing.

Transept traversal

Mechanics

  1. Move across the platforms carefully.
  2. Watch the wall launchers before jumping.
  3. Call out safe timing for players behind you.
  4. Regroup before entering the Sisters encounter.
8

Ir Halak & Ir Anûk

The Sisters encounter teaches the plate and torn-between-dimensions mechanics used again in Oryx. Players build platforms, collect the Brand, then damage the active Sister.

Sisters encounter
Sisters guide

Mechanics

  1. Assign one player to each plate.
  2. Find the starting plate and build the platform sequence.
  3. The torn player jumps across the platforms to collect the Brand.
  4. Use the Brand to protect the team during damage.
  5. Damage the active Sister, then repeat on the other Sister.
9

Oryx, the Taken King

Oryx combines plate mechanics, torn players, ogre bombs, Knight control, damage timing, and final stand execution.

Oryx encounter
Oryx guide

Roles

Plate Players

Stand on assigned plates and build the platform path for the torn player.

Ogre / Knight Killers

Kill Light-Eater Ogres and Knights so bombs remain safe for detonation.

Torn Player

Runs the platform path and steals the Brand from the Vessel.

Mechanics

  1. Assign plate positions before starting.
  2. Kill Ogres as they spawn and leave their bombs on the ground.
  3. Kill Light-Eater Knights before they consume bombs.
  4. Build the plate path for the torn player.
  5. Torn player steals the Brand from the Vessel.
  6. Step into bombs on the countdown, then return to the aura for safety.
  7. Damage Oryx by shooting his exposed chest.
  8. Repeat until final stand, then detonate remaining bombs and finish Oryx.
Countdown Rule

Bomb players should step into bombs together, then immediately return to the protective aura. A messy countdown is one of the easiest ways to lose the Oryx encounter.
