01
The Abyss
Opening

Navigate a pitch-black cavern filled with Hive. The only light comes from Lanterns scattered throughout. Moving outside lamp radius causes instant death.

Tips & Notes
  • Assign a front runner to grab the first lantern and call the path
  • Lamps have a time limit — move quickly between them
  • Splitting into two rows (left and right of the runner) helps avoid traffic jams on lantern swaps
02
Bridge Encounter
Encounter 1

Use the Relic Sword to build a bridge across the abyss and escort three Swordbearers to the other side.

Sword Bearer
Picks up the Sword Relic and uses it to build the bridge by killing Swordbearers
Left
Guards left side from Shriekers and Wizards
Right
Guards right side — mirrors left side
Ogre Killers
Kill the Ogres that spawn when the bridge completes
Tips & Notes
  • Do NOT die while holding the Sword — it disappears and resets the bridge
  • Crotas Swordbearer on the far side must be killed before your team can cross
  • The bridge encounter becomes trivial once the sword rotation is practiced
03
Ir Yût, the Deathsinger
Encounter 2

Kill all Hive Wizards and Knights before Ir Yût completes her Deathsong. Once the song completes the encounter wipes.

  1. Clear outer Wizards and Knights simultaneously in both small rooms
  2. Kill the inner Wizards in the main chamber
  3. All enemies dead = Ir Yût becomes vulnerable
  4. Burn Ir Yût before the Deathsong completes
Tips & Notes
  • Assign pairs to each Wizard location — solo runs waste time
  • Ir Yût's Deathsong timer is generous but not infinite — do not respawn at orbit during this encounter
  • Thundercrash or other burst supers can skip straight to killing Ir Yût if done fast enough
04
Crota, Son of Oryx
Final Boss

The King's son. Crota kneels when his shield is fully drained — the Sword holder must rush in and attack his knee before he stands.

Sword Bearer
Picks up the Sword from the Swordbearer Knight — rushes Crota during the shield-down window and attacks his knee
Shield Drainers
Shoot Crota's energy shield down — coordinate a simultaneous barrage
Add Clear
Kill the Swordbearer Knights fast to ensure the Sword Bearer always has a fresh Sword
  1. Kill a Swordbearer Knight to spawn the Sword Relic
  2. Drainers shoot Crota's shield — coordinate a simultaneous barrage
  3. Shield drops — Sword Bearer rushes and slashes Crota's knee (two R2 hits per cycle)
  4. Sword expires — Sword Bearer backs off and waits for the next Swordbearer
  5. Repeat until Crota dies
Tips & Notes
  • The Sword only lasts about 3 attacks before the debuff kills you — back off before it expires
  • Ogre spawns require immediate attention — they can suppress the entire team
  • Crota stands back up faster after each stagger — speed up your rotations as the fight goes on
