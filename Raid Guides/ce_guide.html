The Abyss

The Abyss is the opening encounter. Players carry the Chalice of Light, become Enlightened, activate lanterns, manage Weight of Darkness, and build the bridge at the end.

The Abyss encounter
The Abyss guide

Core Mechanics

  1. Pick up the Chalice of Light and pass it before the timer runs out.
  2. Players who fill the Chalice become Enlightened.
  3. Use Enlightened to activate lanterns and remove Weight of Darkness.
  4. Move carefully from lantern to lantern while clearing Thrall.
  5. At the end, use Enlightened players to build the bridge.
  6. Cross together and finish the final plate section.
Team Tip

Do not let one player hold the Chalice too long. Call out take and enlightened clearly so the chain never breaks.

2

Bridge

The Bridge encounter uses the Chalice, Enlightened players, bridge plate control, swordbearers, gatekeepers, and coordinated crossings.

Bridge encounter
Bridge guide 1
Bridge guide 2

Roles

Chalice Carrier

Rotates the Chalice and creates Enlightened players.

Sword Bearer

Picks up the Hive sword and kills Gatekeepers across the bridge.

Plate Holders

Hold bridge and side plates to keep the bridge active.

Mechanics

  1. Rotate the Chalice to create Enlightened players.
  2. Use Enlightened to claim the sword when Swordbearers are killed.
  3. Build and hold the bridge using the center plate and side plates.
  4. Send sword players across to kill Gatekeepers.
  5. Rotate more players across until the team is split correctly.
  6. Clear the final enemies and finish the encounter.

Descent

The Descent is the traversal section after Bridge. Use the guide image to keep the team together and avoid missed route calls.

Descent guide

Mechanics

  1. Move carefully through the descent path.
  2. Regroup before difficult jumps or enemy-heavy rooms.
  3. Use the route image if players get separated.
  4. Prepare loadouts before starting Ir Yût.
4

Ir Yût, the Deathsinger

Ir Yût is a timing and communication encounter. Players use the Chalice to become Enlightened, enter towers, kill Wizards, and then damage Ir Yût before the Deathsong completes.

Ir Yût encounter
Ir Yût guide
Ir Yût brand rotation

Roles

Chalice Team

Passes Chalice and creates Enlightened players for tower entry.

Wizard Killers

Use Enlightened to enter rooms and kill assigned Wizards quickly.

DPS Team

Groups for damage once Ir Yût becomes vulnerable.

Mechanics

  1. Build Enlightened players using the Chalice.
  2. Assign players to Wizard rooms.
  3. Kill Wizards quickly and call when your room is clear.
  4. Once enough Wizards are dead, Ir Yût becomes vulnerable.
  5. Group for damage and burn Ir Yût before Deathsong completes.
  6. Repeat with faster rotations if another phase is needed.
5

Crota, Son of Oryx

Crota is the final boss. Players rotate the Chalice, use Enlightened to claim swords, break Crota's shield, sword damage Crota, and survive Oversoul.

Crota encounter
Crota guide
Crota brand rotation

Roles

Chalice Carrier

Creates Enlightened players and maintains the rotation.

Sword Player

Uses Enlightened to take a sword and damage Crota when he kneels.

Oversoul Player

Shoots Oversoul when required so the team does not wipe.

Mechanics

  1. Rotate the Chalice until sword players are Enlightened.
  2. Kill Swordbearers and have Enlightened players claim swords.
  3. Break Crota's shield as a team.
  4. Sword players damage Crota while he kneels.
  5. Assigned player shoots Oversoul before the wipe.
  6. Repeat until final stand, then finish Crota before Oversoul ends the run.
Final Stand

Keep one player ready for Oversoul and make sure sword players are positioned before Crota kneels. Late sword movement can cost the clear.
