Desolation

Desolation is the opening sparrow route through the Europa storm. Players move from shelter bubble to shelter bubble while avoiding Frostbite stacks.

Desolation traversal
Sparrow route guide

Mechanics

  1. Move between shelter bubbles to remove Frostbite.
  2. Do not stay outside too long or Frostbite will kill you.
  3. Follow the sparrow path and regroup at each safe zone.
  4. Open the route forward once the team reaches the final shelter.
Team Tip

New players should follow one confident sparrow lead. Getting split up in the storm is the easiest way to lose time.

2

Crypt Security

Crypt Security introduces Deep Stone Crypt's augment system. Scanner reads correct panels, Operator shoots controls, and the team transfers augments through terminals.

Crypt Security encounter
Crypt Security guide

Roles

Scanner

Reads which panels are glowing and calls them out clearly.

Operator

Shoots the correct panels/buttons based on Scanner calls.

Add Clear

Keeps Servitors, Vandals, and adds under control while augments rotate.

Mechanics

  1. Split into light side and dark side teams.
  2. Scanner identifies the correct underground panels.
  3. Operator goes below and shoots the panels called by Scanner.
  4. Transfer Scanner upstairs for fuse callouts.
  5. Damage the correct fuses in the order Scanner calls.
  6. Repeat until Crypt Security is destroyed.
3

Atraks-1

Atraks-1 splits the fireteam between Europa and the space station. Scanner identifies the correct Atraks clone, the team bursts it down, and players manage Replication debuffs before they expire.

Atraks-1 encounter
Atraks-1 guide

Roles

Scanner

Identifies the correct Atraks clone to damage.

Operator

Shoots Replication off players and opens airlocks when needed.

Damage Teams

Split between top and bottom and burst the correct clone immediately.

Mechanics

  1. Split into top and bottom teams.
  2. Scanner calls the glowing Atraks clone.
  3. Damage only the correct clone.
  4. Pick up Replication after damage and manage the timer.
  5. Operator shoots Replication off players in the correct location.
  6. Repeat until final stand, then quickly kill the correct copies.
Important

Atraks is a burst-damage encounter. Save heavy ammo and supers for the exact clone call instead of spreading damage randomly.

4

Descent

Descent is the nuclear core encounter aboard the Morning Star. Players manage Scanner, Operator, and Suppressor augments while dunking nuclear cores into the correct bins.

Descent encounter

Roles

Scanner

Calls the correct dunk bins for nuclear cores.

Operator

Opens doors and frees trapped core carriers.

Suppressor

Suppresses Taniks by standing under drones and shooting him.

Mechanics

  1. Pick up augments and assign Scanner, Operator, and Suppressor.
  2. Kill enemies until nuclear cores spawn.
  3. Scanner calls the correct bins.
  4. Core carriers pick up nukes and dunk them in the called bins.
  5. Operator frees trapped players if a core carrier gets detained.
  6. Suppressor stuns Taniks from the drone positions.
  7. Repeat until all dunk phases are complete.
5

Taniks Reborn

Taniks Reborn continues the augment and nuclear core flow while increasing pressure on the team. Clean role discipline matters more than raw damage here.

Taniks Reborn encounter
Taniks Reborn guide

Mechanics

  1. Keep Scanner, Operator, and Suppressor roles assigned.
  2. Use Scanner to identify correct dunk locations.
  3. Carry nuclear cores to the called bins.
  4. Operator handles detain/trapped player mechanics.
  5. Suppressor stuns Taniks from the correct drone positions.
  6. Deposit cores cleanly and prepare for the final crash transition.
6

Taniks, the Abomination

Taniks is the final boss. The team shoots Taniks' engines to create nuclear cores, carries them to the correct bins, handles detain, suppresses Taniks, and then groups for damage.

Taniks final boss encounter
Taniks guide

Roles

Scanner

Calls which bins are active for nuclear core deposits.

Operator

Shoots detain bubbles off core carriers.

Suppressor

Suppresses Taniks from drone positions to allow dunking.

Mechanics

  1. Damage Taniks' engine thrusters to drop nuclear cores.
  2. Core carriers pick up nukes and move to the called bins.
  3. Operator frees any detained players immediately.
  4. Suppressor stuns Taniks from the drone positions.
  5. Dunk cores in correct bins.
  6. Group in the damage zone and DPS Taniks.
  7. Repeat until final stand, then finish him quickly.
Damage Tip

Group cleanly for damage and avoid standing too close to Taniks' debris field. Clear callouts are more important than rushing the dunk.
