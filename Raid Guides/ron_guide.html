Crossing the Chasm

This traversal section introduces the Light and Dark node flow. Players use refuge, launchers, and node connections to cross the chasm.

Crossing the Chasm

Mechanics

  1. Move through the route while using Light and Dark node connections.
  2. Use launchers carefully and wait for the team when needed.
  3. Create refuge if the wipe pulse is approaching.
  4. Regroup before starting Cataclysm.
2

Cataclysm

Cataclysm is the first encounter. The runner connects Light nodes while the rest of the team kills Psions, Tormentors, and enemies to extend the Sweeping Terror timer.

Cataclysm encounter
Cataclysm guide

Roles

Runner

Connects Light nodes in order and keeps the encounter moving.

Add Clear / Tormentor Team

Kills Psions, spawns Tormentors, and defeats Tormentors to extend the timer.

Mechanics

  1. Runner shoots the starting Light node to gain the Field of Light buff.
  2. Runner connects the next glowing node in the chain.
  3. Add clear kills Psions to spawn Tormentors.
  4. Kill Tormentors quickly to extend Sweeping Terror.
  5. Repeat node chain sections until the encounter completes.
3

Scission

Scission splits the team across two sides. Runners connect Light and Dark nodes while players use launchers to cross the gap and maintain progress on both sides.

Scission encounter
Scission guide

Roles

Light Runner

Connects Field of Light nodes.

Dark Runner

Connects Flux of Darkness nodes.

Add Clear

Kills shielded enemies after getting the correct buff.

Mechanics

  1. Split into left and right groups.
  2. Runners connect Light and Dark node chains.
  3. Players use launchers to cross between sides when needed.
  4. Use the correct buff to kill shielded enemies.
  5. Complete all floors before the timer ends.
4

Macrocosm

Macrocosm is the planet encounter. Players swap Light and Dark planets between plates, then match planets in the center to start damage.

Macrocosm encounter
Macrocosm guide

Roles

Planet Runners

Identify incorrect planets and move them to the matching side.

Colossus Killers

Kill Lieutenants/Colossi to reveal which planets need to move.

DPS Team

Groups for damage on the correct center plates.

Mechanics

  1. Kill Colossi to reveal planet alignment.
  2. Call out which planet on each plate is wrong.
  3. Pick up and swap planets between Light and Dark sides.
  4. Match the center planets for damage order.
  5. Stand on the correct plate color and damage Zo'aurc.
  6. Repeat until the boss is defeated.
5

Nezarec, Final God of Pain

Nezarec is the final boss. Runners complete Light and Dark node chains, one player manages Nezarec's hatred, and the team creates refuge when needed before damage.

Nezarec encounter
Nezarec guide

Roles

Light Runner

Completes Field of Light node chain.

Dark Runner

Completes Flux of Darkness node chain.

Hatred Holder

Controls Nezarec's aggression and calls when refuge is needed.

Mechanics

  1. Light and Dark runners begin their node chains.
  2. Hatred holder shoots Nezarec's weak points to control his attention.
  3. Identify the wipe color and create the correct refuge if needed.
  4. Complete both node chains to start damage.
  5. Group for DPS and avoid Nezarec's slam attacks.
  6. Repeat until final stand, then finish Nezarec quickly.
Refuge Rule

Use Light Refuge to survive a Dark wipe pulse and Dark Refuge to survive a Light wipe pulse. Call the color early so players have time to move.
