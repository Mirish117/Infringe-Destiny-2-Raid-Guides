1

Monolith Approach

Monolith Approach is the opening traversal section into the Witness's monolith. The route teaches visual language, traversal spacing, and preparation for the resonance encounters.

Monolith Approach

Mechanics

  1. Move through the approach route while clearing enemies.
  2. Watch for hidden deepsight rooms and secret chest routes.
  3. Call out resonance shapes and visual clues when players are learning the raid.
  4. Regroup before starting Substratum.
2

Substratum

Substratum introduces resonance shapes, plates, conductors, and split-team room clearing. The team must keep the timer alive while using matching resonance to complete conduits.

Substratum guide

Roles

Left Team

Clears one path of rooms and communicates resonance/conductor status.

Right Team

Clears the other path of rooms and keeps pace with the opposite team.

Shape Caller

Tracks which resonance shape is needed for each conductor.

Mechanics

  1. Split into two teams of three.
  2. Clear rooms and defeat key enemies to open the next route.
  3. Collect matching resonance shapes without over-stacking.
  4. Use plate timing and conductor shots to charge the correct nodes.
  5. Bank resonance when needed to extend the timer.
  6. Repeat until the encounter completes.
Resonance Rule

Do not grab random shapes. Carry only the shape your conductor requires, and avoid taking too many stacks unless you are ready to deposit.

3

Dissipation / Herald of Finality

Dissipation is the Herald of Finality boss encounter. The team handles resonance/conductor mechanics while controlling the boss and preparing for damage phases.

Dissipation guide
Herald of Finality

Roles

Conductor Players

Handle resonance shape matching and conductor completion.

Boss Control

Keeps the Herald positioned and survives suppression attacks.

Add Clear

Controls adds so mechanic players can complete resonance safely.

Mechanics

  1. Assign players to conductor positions.
  2. Collect the correct resonance shapes and complete conductor interactions.
  3. Clear adds and manage boss pressure while conduits are completed.
  4. When the damage window opens, group and burn the Herald.
  5. Repeat until the Herald is defeated.
4

Repository

Repository builds on the resonance and plate timing systems. The arena is larger, more punishing, and requires cleaner shape communication.

Repository encounter
Repository guide
Glyph wipe patterns

Mechanics

  1. Split into assigned sides or plate groups.
  2. Identify required shapes for each conductor.
  3. Collect and bank correct resonance.
  4. Use plate timing to complete conductor sequences.
  5. Clear dangerous enemies quickly so resonance players can move safely.
  6. Repeat until all required sequences are complete.
5

Verity

Verity is a communication-heavy shape encounter. Players inside solo rooms and outside in the main room must identify, pass, and build the correct 2D/3D shape combinations.

Verity encounter
Verity guide
Verity shape example 1
Verity shape example 2

Roles

Inside Players

Read statues, identify needed shapes, and communicate clearly to outside players.

Outside Players

Build the correct shape combinations on statues based on inside callouts.

Mechanics

  1. Inside players identify their statue and call needed shapes.
  2. Outside players kill enemies and collect shapes.
  3. Outside team distributes shapes to match the required combinations.
  4. Inside players prepare their rooms and confirm when ready.
  5. Complete shape matching to escape and move into the next cycle.
  6. Repeat until the encounter is complete.
Communication Rule

Use simple calls: circle, triangle, and square. Do not let multiple people talk over the inside callouts.

6

The Witness

The Witness is the final encounter. Players dodge hand attacks, read attack patterns, break glyphs, gain resonance, and damage the Witness during the exposed phase.

The Witness encounter

Mechanics

  1. Assign players to handle hand/glyph mechanics and add control.
  2. Read the Witness attack pattern and dodge the floor attacks.
  3. Break the correct hand/glyph interactions to progress the phase.
  4. Gain and manage resonance safely.
  5. When damage starts, stand in the correct area and damage the Witness while dodging attacks.
  6. Repeat until final stand and finish the boss quickly.
DPS Rule

Damage is only clean if the team survives the attack pattern. Prioritize dodging first, then damage. Dead players do no DPS.
