01
Monolith Approach
Traversal

Navigate the exterior of the Witness's monolith structure. The path winds up the side of the raid's alien architecture.

Recommended Loadout
  • Any movement exotics — Eager Edge for fast traversal
  • Ranged weapons to deal with snipers along the path
Tips & Notes
  • The deepsight red border chest requires activating pylons in hidden rooms 1, 3, and 5 — look for glowing conductor plates on your way through
  • Watch the third column from the left in the first enclosed chamber — its shape tells you which resonance room symbols correspond to which rooms
02
Substratum
Encounter 1

A 9x9 grid of interconnected rooms. Split into two teams of three and navigate Vex-filled chambers by clearing enemies and charging conduits with resonance buffs.

Roles
Team Alpha
Takes one of the two starting doors that open from the central chamber. Clears rooms sequentially.
Team Bravo
Takes the second opening door. Clears rooms in parallel with Team Alpha.
Floater
Either team can designate a caller to track conduit shapes and communicate across fireteam.
Mechanic: Resonance

Resonance pickups spawn as geometric shapes — Triangle (Pyramidal), Circle (Spherical), Square (Hexahedron). Carry up to 3 stacks; a 4th kills you instantly. Depositing resonance into a conduit requires matching the shape to what the conduit displays.

Flow
  1. Pick up triangle resonance in the starting room and bank it to reveal The Witness's hand
  2. Two doors open — each team enters one and clears Vex
  3. Kill the Quantum Minotaur in your room to spawn a Hydra in the opposing team's room
  4. Clear the Hydra to open the next door for the opposite team
  5. Room 3 contains an Overload Minotaur Champion — kill it to spawn the Threshold Sentinel Tormentor in the central room
  6. Kill the Tormentor, then charge the two unlocked conduit pairs with matching resonance and step on the plate at the right moment
Tips & Notes
  • Do not step on a plate too early or too late — wait for the energy pulse to reach the plate before stepping
  • If a conduit is locked, all resonance from that plate pair disappears — communicate clearly
  • Doors always open from north/south/east/west first, then a corner direction second
03
Herald of Finality
Boss Encounter

A large Tormentor-class boss. The encounter requires maintaining resonance conduits while simultaneously dealing damage and avoiding the boss's suppression attacks.

Recommended Loadout
  • High-damage primaries for add clear
  • Heavy weapons for DPS — linear fusion rifles or rockets
  • Anti-Overload mods required
Tips & Notes
  • The Herald suppresses on heavy attacks — maintain distance and bait the lunge
  • Assign one player per conduit pair so nobody doubles up on resonance
  • Damage phase begins after all conduits in the current round are fully charged
04
Warren
Encounter 2

The mechanic from Substratum evolves — all three resonance shapes are now in play simultaneously. Teams must deposit resonance into banks faster while managing tougher enemies.

Flow
  1. Split 3-3 as before but now coordinate Pyramidal, Spherical, and Hexahedron resonances
  2. Each conduit displays a shape — only the player with the matching resonance can activate it
  3. Shoot the conduit shape while a teammate steps on the plate — timing is everything
  4. Once all conduits are charged, bank resonance at the main bank to reset the timer
Tips & Notes
  • Call out your shape clearly — 'Triangle left', 'Circle right'
  • Never carry more than 2 stacks unless you are about to deposit immediately
  • If you pick up a different shape it overwrites your current shape — be intentional
05
Repository
Encounter 3

The most mechanically complex encounter before the final boss. All three shapes are active and the team must manage multiple plates and conduits simultaneously across a much larger arena.

Flow
  1. The fireteam splits into two groups responsible for opposite sides of the arena
  2. Each side activates a conduit by matching resonance and timing plate steps
  3. As conduits charge, heavier enemies spawn — clear them without losing your resonance stack
  4. Bank excess resonance at the central bank to extend the timer
Tips & Notes
  • Call dead conduits immediately so your partner can re-unlock them
  • Tormentors prioritize whoever has the most resonance stacks — kite them around pillars
  • This encounter has the most punishing timer — efficiency matters more than perfection
06
Verity
Encounter 4

A solo-mechanic encounter. Each of the six Guardians is placed in a solo room with a Subjugator and a set of 3D geometric shapes displayed on a statue.

Solo Room
  1. Kill the Subjugator in your room
  2. Observe which shapes your statue is NOT holding — that is your shape to contribute to the central team
  3. Shoot the correct shape on the central statue outside your room while teammates do the same
Inside vs Outside

Three Guardians are locked inside solo rooms. Three are working the outside arena. Outside players dunk shapes to deposit them while inside players communicate what's needed.

Inside
Kill subjugator, read your missing shape, shoot it on the outside
Outside
Receive shapes from inside players, dunk them on the statues while fighting the main boss
Tips & Notes
  • Communication is everything — inside players must call their missing shape the moment their subjugator dies
  • Outside players: never deposit a shape that wasn't called for
  • The encounter resets if the outside boss is not defeated in time
07
The Witness
Final Boss

The raid's climactic encounter. The Witness attacks the fireteam across a collapsing arena while mechanics from all previous encounters combine.

Recommended Loadout
  • Best-in-slot DPS weapons — rockets with autoload + surplus or linear fusions
  • Healing grenades and Well of Radiance recommended
  • Resistance mods for the specific damage type the Witness uses in the current rotation
Phase 1 — Geometric Shapes

Mechanics mirror Verity — match and deposit shapes on the correct pylons to open the damage phase. Each incorrect shape slows progress and extends the encounter.

Damage Phase

Unload everything into the Witness's weak point. The window closes quickly — coordinate supers and heavy ammo for maximum output.

Phase 2 — Enrage

After surviving enough damage phases, the Witness begins its enrage sequence. The final damage phase must bring it to zero or the raid wipes.

Tips & Notes
  • Assign a lead caller to manage shape distribution in Phase 1
  • Save your super for the opening seconds of each damage phase — Supers do the most DPS in the first 5 seconds
  • The second secret chest is in the traversal section just before this fight — check the bottom-right wall near the large door
