Acquisition

Acquisition introduces symbol reading, obelisks, rooms, Taken Knights, and coordinated symbol shooting. Players read symbols, identify matching rooms, kill Knights, and shoot correct obelisk symbols.

Acquisition encounter
Acquisition guide

Roles

Obelisk Defenders

Defend assigned obelisks and read symbols that appear.

Runners

Enter rooms, kill Taken Knights, and bring back symbol information.

Symbol Caller

Combines callouts and confirms which obelisk symbols to shoot.

Mechanics

  1. Split into three obelisk groups.
  2. Read the symbol that tells the team which room to enter.
  3. Runner enters the correct room and kills the Taken Knight.
  4. Runner calls the symbol found inside.
  5. Repeat until the team has the required symbol set.
  6. Shoot the correct matching obelisk symbols together.

The Caretaker

Caretaker combines boss control, symbol collection, obelisk deposits, stun timing, and multi-floor damage phases.

Caretaker encounter
Caretaker floor 1
Caretaker floor 2
Caretaker floor 3

Roles

Runners

Enter the dark room, collect symbols, and return to shoot/deposit at the obelisk.

Stunners

Stun Caretaker by shooting his face and back when he opens.

Add Clear

Controls enemies and keeps runners/stunners alive.

Mechanics

  1. Runners collect symbols from the dark room.
  2. Runners return and shoot matching symbols on the obelisk.
  3. Stunners stop Caretaker from reaching the obelisk.
  4. When the obelisk is complete, group for plate-based damage.
  5. Move up each floor and repeat until final stand.
Damage Rule

Damage happens plate by plate. Do not leave a plate too early, and move as a team when the next plate activates.

3

Exhibition

Exhibition is a relic relay encounter. Players rotate relics, kill Glyphkeepers, read symbols, and open doors before the timer expires.

Exhibition encounter
Exhibition guide

Roles

Relic Holders

Carry relics, cleanse teammates, destroy Taken blights, or shield the team depending on relic type.

Glyph Readers

Kill Glyphkeepers and call the shared symbol needed to open the door.

Add Clear

Kills enemies quickly so relic holders and readers can move.

Mechanics

  1. Pick up the starting relic and begin the timer.
  2. Kill Glyphkeepers on both sides and call symbols.
  3. Identify the shared symbol and shoot/open the correct door.
  4. Rotate relics between rooms so no player is locked out by cooldown.
  5. Use relic abilities to cleanse, shield, or destroy mechanics as needed.
  6. Repeat through all rooms before the timer expires.

Rhulk, Disciple of the Witness

Rhulk is the final boss. The encounter uses leeching force, emanating force, symbol reading, dunking, weak point breaks, and mobile boss damage.

Rhulk encounter
Rhulk guide

Roles

Splitters

Manage Leeching Force and split the buff between players.

Dunkers

Convert Leeching Force into Emanating Force and dunk at the correct symbol.

Symbol Readers

Read the correct dunk symbols and call the matching location.

Mechanics

  1. Start by gaining Leeching Force from Rhulk's beam.
  2. Split the buff so multiple players can progress mechanics.
  3. Dunkers stand in Rhulk's beam to convert into Emanating Force.
  4. Readers call the correct dunk symbols.
  5. Dunk Emanating Force at the matching locations to push the phase.
  6. In damage, break Rhulk's weak points and keep moving while damaging him.
  7. Repeat until final stand and finish Rhulk quickly.
DPS Rule

Rhulk moves aggressively during damage. Prioritize staying alive and repositioning cleanly instead of standing still and getting kicked.
