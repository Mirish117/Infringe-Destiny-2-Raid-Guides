01
Recon
Opening

Traverse Europa's snowfields while staying inside the safety of light pylons. Players take turns activating the pylons to advance.

Tips & Notes
  • The debuff 'Frostbite' stacks outside pylons — move quickly between them
  • One player can activate multiple pylons in sequence — assign a lead runner
  • The hidden caches along the route are worth hunting for extra ammo
02
Crypt Security
Encounter 1

Split the fireteam between two separated rooms — Upstairs and Downstairs. Players in each room must charge their Scanner and Operator augments and share information across rooms.

Mechanic: Augments

Two augments exist — Scanner (glows yellow) and Operator (glows red). The Scanner can read information. The Operator can interact with devices. Augments can only be transferred by shooting a specific Augment Terminal.

Scanner
Reads the active column of lamps in your room and calls it out to the Operator across the team
Operator
Shoots the correct column panel based on the Scanner's callout
  1. Downstairs Scanner reads which of the three columns is lit
  2. Calls it to the Upstairs Operator — e.g. 'Left column'
  3. Upstairs Operator shoots the corresponding panel
  4. Augments transfer when the Operator shoots the Scanner's terminal
  5. Repeat until both sides are charged — then damage phase
Tips & Notes
  • Transfer augments before they expire — the debuff kills the holder
  • Assign permanent Upstairs and Downstairs teams before the encounter begins
  • The order of columns to charge is random each attempt — always re-read fresh
03
Atraks-1
Encounter 2

Fight four copies of Atraks-1 spread across two floors. Only the correct copy in each room can be damaged — identified by which copy is glowing.

Structure

Three copies of Atraks are on each floor (six total). One on each floor is vulnerable at a time. You damage the correct copies and then dunk their Replication core before time expires.

Top (3)
Fight the three Atraks copies in the space station above — find and damage the glowing one
Bottom (3)
Fight the three Atraks on the lower floor — damage the glowing copy
Dunk Runner
After damaging a copy, collect the Replication and dunk it in the trash chute before time expires
Tips & Notes
  • The glowing Atraks is never the same one two rounds in a row — reset your eyes each phase
  • Dunking the Replication in the wrong chute on the wrong floor does nothing
  • In the final phase, all four Atraks go vulnerable simultaneously — all four must be dunked quickly
04
Taniks
Final Boss

Taniks, The Abomination — combines the Replication mechanic with a nuclear core and a rotating vulnerability pattern across four engine pods.

Recommended Loadout
  • Best DPS — Lament exotic sword is exceptional here
  • Stasis for shutting down Taniks's movement during nuke phase
  • Well of Radiance for DPS phases
Phase 1 — Augments
  1. Kill adds to generate Scanner and Operator augments
  2. Scanner identifies which two of the four engine pods are glowing
  3. Operator shoots the glowing pods to open them
  4. Relic Runner grabs the nuclear core from a killed Abomination
  5. Dunk the core into one of the two open pods
Phase 2 — DPS

After two cores are dunked, Taniks kneels. All six players focus DPS on his engine weak points.

Tips & Notes
  • The Nuke phase activates if cores aren't dunked fast enough — hold still and survive it
  • Dunking wrong cores causes a wipe — always confirm the Scanner's call before dunking
  • In Challenge Mode: each player may only dunk one core per damage phase
