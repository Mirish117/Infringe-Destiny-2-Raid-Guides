The Descent
Opening / Traversal
The Descent Guide

Navigate the Dreaming City's cursed spires and Taken pathways. Move carefully through the vertical sections, clear Taken enemies, and keep the fireteam grouped while progressing toward the first major encounter.

Tips & Notes
  • Move steadily — the path is linear but has several vertical sections that can disorient players.
  • Taken Phalanxes are a major threat near ledges, so clear them before jumping across exposed paths.
  • Use the guide image above as your route reference for the opening traversal.
02
Erebus
Encounter 1
Erebus Guide

Work through the symbol route and Taken enemies. Use the encounter map to identify the correct path, callouts, and route order.

  1. Read the symbol or route callout for the next area.
  2. Move through the correct path while clearing Taken enemies.
  3. Stay together so nobody gets isolated or knocked off by Taken Phalanxes.
  4. Repeat the route process until the encounter is complete.
Tips & Notes
  • Call the route before the team moves.
  • Use short callouts so the fireteam can react quickly.
  • If someone gets lost, regroup before continuing deeper into the route.
03
Vorgeth, the Boundless Hunger
Encounter 2
Vorgeth Guide

Vorgeth is the Taken Ogre encounter. Clear Wizards, collect Petitioner's Mark, dunk to remove Vorgeth's shield, then burn the boss during the damage window.

  1. Clear Taken enemies and weaken the Wizards around the arena.
  2. Kill Wizards to collect Petitioner's Mark stacks.
  3. Deposit the completed Petitioner's Burden at a brazier to drop Vorgeth's shield.
  4. Damage Vorgeth until the shield returns or the boss is defeated.
  5. Repeat the cycle if another phase is needed.
Tips & Notes
  • Do not kill Wizards too early if the team is not ready to collect and dunk.
  • Clear adds before the dunk so the damage phase is safer.
  • Use burst damage, Well of Radiance, or survivability tools for a cleaner burn phase.
04
Dul Incaru, the Eternal Return
Final Boss
Dul Incaru Guide

Dul Incaru is the final boss of The Shattered Throne. Kill the Knights to gain damage buffs, avoid being overwhelmed by Taken enemies, and burn Dul Incaru before the buff expires.

Recommended Loadout
  • High burst DPS weapon for the boss damage window.
  • Strong add-clear weapon for Taken Psions and Thrall.
  • Well of Radiance, Tether, or survivability tools for safer execution.
  1. Clear Taken adds and control the arena.
  2. Damage the Knights evenly so they can be killed close together.
  3. Kill the Knights and collect the damage buffs they drop.
  4. With buffs active, focus damage on Dul Incaru.
  5. If the boss survives, reset and repeat the Knight phase.
Tips & Notes
  • Killing the Knights close together maximizes the time your full damage buff is active.
  • Keep distance from the Knights because they can delete players quickly at close range.
  • Clear Psions before committing to boss damage so the team is not overwhelmed mid-DPS.
