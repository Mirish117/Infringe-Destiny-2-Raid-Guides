01
Flooded Rig
Opening

Traverse a partially submerged oil rig overrun by Hive. Darkness zones are scattered throughout — stay in the light of the glowing jellyfish-like creatures.

Tips & Notes
  • The bioluminescent creatures mark safe zones — follow them through the dark sections.
  • Diving underwater is fatal outside the safe zones — the Taken corruption deals rapid damage.
02
Hive Ritual
Encounter 1
The Ritual Guide

Three ritual circles must be purified in sequence. Each circle requires a Ghost to be sacrificed within it while the fireteam holds off Hive.

Ghost Bearers 1-2
Carry the Ghost to each ritual circle in sequence.
Defenders 1-2
Hold the surrounding area free of Hive while the ritual completes.
  1. Pick up the Ghost near the start of the encounter.
  2. Carry it to the first glowing ritual circle and stand inside.
  3. Hold off Hive until the ritual completes.
  4. Move to the second and third circles and repeat.
Tips & Notes
  • The Ghost carrier cannot shoot — protect them aggressively.
  • Shriekers spawn at specific points during each ritual — prioritize killing them immediately.
  • The Ghost's timer does not pause when carrying — keep moving.
03
Ecthar, Shield of Savathûn
Encounter 2
Ecthar Guide

Ecthar patrols between three pools of Deep Sight. Use the Finisher mechanic to damage him — regular weapons do nothing until his shield is broken by a Finisher.

  1. Kill adds until you build full Finisher energy.
  2. Approach Ecthar and trigger the Finisher — this strips his shield.
  3. All three players dump DPS while he is exposed.
  4. He retreats to a new pool — repeat.
Tips & Notes
  • Finishers can be chained if energy is managed correctly — avoid using them on adds unnecessarily.
  • Ecthar has a massive shockwave attack when retreating — back up before the retreat animation completes.
  • Divinity makes his damage phase significantly easier to maximize.
04
Simmumah ur-Nokris
Final Boss
Simmumah Ur Nokru Guide

The dungeon's final boss — a massive Hive Wizard. The Ghost mechanic returns combined with a complex symbol puzzle.

Recommended Loadout
  • Linears or rockets for DPS phases.
  • Divinity strongly recommended.
  • Well of Radiance inside the damage bubble.
  1. Kill adds to generate Symbol knowledge — read the three symbols displayed.
  2. Carry the Ghost to the correct three columns matching the symbols.
  3. All three columns activated = boss vulnerable.
  4. Dump DPS — boss retreats after the window closes.
  5. Repeat until dead.
Tips & Notes
  • The Ghost carrier is marked — Simmumah targets them preferentially.
  • Having all three symbols memorized before moving saves critical seconds.
  • Solo: managing the Ghost carry and reading symbols simultaneously is the core challenge — practice the read while moving.
