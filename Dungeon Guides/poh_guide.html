Necropolis
Opening
Necropolis Guide

A traversal section through Hive tunnels. Three runes on a central spire must be identified then found and destroyed on the dungeon's outer wall.

  1. Observe the three rune symbols on the central spire.
  2. Navigate the outer wall and find the three matching rune symbols carved into the wall.
  3. Destroy each matching rune by using the Swords near each one.
  4. All three destroyed — the inner gate opens.
Tips & Notes
  • Each rune has a specific weapon interaction — Sword slashes, not primary fire.
  • The Swords here are the Hive Blades — pick them up from the Swordbearer Knight near each rune.
  • Wrong runes do not visibly fail — you simply will not progress until the correct three are destroyed.
02
Tunnels of Despair
Encounter 1
Tunnel of Despair Guide

A labyrinthine encounter in the Hive tunnels. Find and kill three Hive Knights to collect Illicit Relics, then deposit them in the central chamber.

  1. Navigate the tunnels — they branch into three paths.
  2. Find a Knight on each path and kill it.
  3. Pick up the Illicit Relic dropped.
  4. Carry the Relic back to the central deposit platform and place it.
  5. All three Relics placed = encounter complete.
Tips & Notes
  • The tunnel paths are one-directional — memorize the correct route to each Knight.
  • Relics expire if held too long — deposit promptly.
  • Ogres patrol the tunnels and are immune to most damage — avoid or dodge past them.
03
Chamber of Suffering
Encounter 2

Hold the central plate while managing waves of Hive enemies. Use the orbs dropped by Heretical Knights to keep the encounter progressing and prevent the wipe mechanic from building too high.

Plate Holder
Keeps pressure on the center plate and prevents the encounter from wiping.
Orb Runner
Kills Knights, collects Void charges, and deposits them at the center.
Add Clear
Controls Thrall, Acolytes, Knights, and boomer pressure.
  1. Stand on the center plate to begin the encounter.
  2. Kill Heretical Knights when they spawn.
  3. Pick up the dropped Void charge and dunk it at the center.
  4. Repeat until enough charges are deposited and the encounter completes.
Tips & Notes
  • Do not leave the plate empty for too long.
  • Prioritize ranged Knights and boomer pressure because they can quickly overwhelm the plate holder.
  • Well of Radiance, healing rifts, barricades, and crowd-control builds are very useful here.
04
The Harrow
Traversal
The Harrow Guide

The Harrow is the rune-door traversal section before Zulmak. Match the correct symbols, move through the correct doorways, and avoid choosing the wrong route through the Hive maze.

  1. Identify the rune symbols shown for the route.
  2. Move through the matching doors in the correct order.
  3. Avoid incorrect doors and unnecessary fights when possible.
  4. Reach the final path that leads to Zulmak.
Tips & Notes
  • Call out each symbol clearly before moving.
  • Stay grouped so nobody gets lost in the maze.
  • Use the image above as the route reference while moving through the Harrow.
05
Zulmak, Instrument of Torment
Final Boss

Zulmak is the final boss of Pit of Heresy. Defeat the three mini-bosses around the arena, use their relic mechanics correctly, dunk the Void charges, then damage Zulmak from the center plate.

Recommended Loadout
  • Strong add-clear weapon for Thrall and Acolytes.
  • Heavy burst damage for Zulmak DPS.
  • Well of Radiance, Ward of Dawn, or strong survivability tools for the center plate.
Mechanic: Relic Mini-Bosses

Each side of the arena has a mini-boss tied to a specific relic attack. Use the correct relic action to defeat each one, collect the Void charge, and dunk it at the center.

  1. Kill the Swordbearer Knight to pick up the Hive Sword.
  2. Use the Sword's correct attack type against the matching mini-boss.
  3. Pick up the Void charge from the defeated mini-boss.
  4. Dunk the charge at the center plate.
  5. After all required charges are dunked, stand on the center plate and damage Zulmak.
  6. Leave the plate before the wipe pulse, then repeat until Zulmak is defeated.
Tips & Notes
  • Do not stay on the center plate after damage ends.
  • Assign one player to watch Zulmak and call when the team needs to leave.
  • Clear adds before dunking the final charge so the DPS setup is cleaner.
