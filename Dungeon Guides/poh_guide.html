01
Necropolis
Opening

A traversal section through Hive tunnels. Three runes on a central spire must be identified then found and destroyed on the dungeon's outer wall.

  1. Observe the three rune symbols on the central spire
  2. Navigate the outer wall — find the three matching rune symbols carved into the wall
  3. Destroy each matching rune by using the Swords near each one
  4. All three destroyed — the inner gate opens
Tips & Notes
  • Each rune has a specific weapon interaction — Sword slashes, not primary fire
  • The Swords here are the Hive Blades — pick them up from the Swordbearer Knight near each rune
  • Wrong runes don't visibly fail — you simply won't progress until the correct three are destroyed
02
Tunnels of Despair
Encounter 1

A labyrinthine encounter in the Hive tunnels. Find and kill three Hive Knights to collect Illicit Relics, then deposit them in the central chamber.

  1. Navigate the tunnels — they branch into three paths
  2. Find a Knight on each path and kill it
  3. Pick up the Illicit Relic dropped
  4. Carry the Relic back to the central deposit platform and place it
  5. All three Relics placed = encounter complete
Tips & Notes
  • The tunnel paths are one-directional — memorize the correct route to each Knight
  • Relics expire if held too long — deposit promptly
  • Ogres patrol the tunnels and are immune to most damage — avoid or dodge past them
03
Chamber of Suffering
Encounter 2

Hold three plates simultaneously while managing a Hive Curse stack mechanic. If the curse reaches maximum the encounter wipes.

Plate Holders (3)
Each holds one plate permanently — swap if curse is too high
Curse Clearer
Kills the Knights that spawn to reset the curse stack
  1. Three players stand on the three plates
  2. Knights spawn continuously — kill them to reduce the Hive Curse
  3. Balancing curse reduction with plate-holding is the core tension
Tips & Notes
  • Never step off a plate unless a teammate is immediately replacing you
  • Assign a primary Knight-killer role — consistency beats chaos
  • Armor mods reducing the Hive Curse damage are recommended if available
04
Zulmak, Instrument of Torment
Final Boss

Zulmak must be damaged using a specific sequence of elemental buffs. Using the wrong elemental damage during the wrong phase resets the encounter.

Recommended Loadout
  • Solar, Arc, and Void weapons — you need all three damage types accessible
  • Anarchy for sustained damage across all phases
  • Well of Radiance for DPS windows
Mechanic: Elemental Rotation

Zulmak cycles through three elemental shields in a fixed order: Solar → Arc → Void. Only the matching elemental damage deals meaningful damage during each phase.

  1. Identify Zulmak's current shield color
  2. Rotate your weapon to match — coordinate with teammates
  3. When the shield breaks, burst your highest-DPS weapon
  4. Shield cycles to next element — swap damage type
Tips & Notes
  • Calling the shield cycle clearly ('Solar up, switching to Arc') prevents wasted ammo
  • In the final phase all three shields cycle rapidly — have all three weapons ready in your slots
  • The secret chest path in the Pit gives Pinnacle gear — worth running
