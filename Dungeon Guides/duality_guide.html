Quick Reference

Optional image slot: ../Dungeon Assets/DUAL Quick Reference.png

Steps

  1. Duality swaps between the normal realm and Nightmare realm using bells.
  2. In Nightmare, stand near the bell before shooting it or you will die.
  3. Kill Bellkeepers to unlock bells during encounters.
Fireteam Tip

Call the mechanic before moving. A clean callout usually saves more time than rushing the next step.

The Mindscape

Steps

  1. Learn bell movement and the difference between normal and Nightmare spaces.
  2. Use bells to open routes and reach the first encounter.

Nightmare of Gahlran

Optional image slot: ../Dungeon Assets/DUAL Nightmare of Gahlran.png

Steps

  1. Identify needed symbols and enter Nightmare to collect Standards.
  2. Kill the correct Standard Bearers, return, and dunk Standards.
  3. Open Gahlran damage by clearing rooms and surviving the Nightmare timer.
Fireteam Tip

Call the mechanic before moving. A clean callout usually saves more time than rushing the next step.

The Crypt

Optional image slot: ../Dungeon Assets/DUAL The Crypt.png

Steps

  1. Traverse between realms, use bells carefully, and avoid being caught outside the safe bell circle.

The Vault

Optional image slot: ../Dungeon Assets/DUAL The Vault.png

Steps

  1. Read the needed symbols, enter Nightmare, kill Standard Bearers, and dunk Standards.
  2. Repeat until the vault opens while managing Bellkeepers and Nightmare timers.
Fireteam Tip

Call the mechanic before moving. A clean callout usually saves more time than rushing the next step.

5

The Depths

Optional image slot: ../Dungeon Assets/DUAL The Depths.png

Steps

  1. Platform through the lower areas and prepare for Caiatl.
  2. Keep the team together because falling or mistiming bells can cost time.
Fireteam Tip

Call the mechanic before moving. A clean callout usually saves more time than rushing the next step.

6

Nightmare of Caiatl

Optional image slot: ../Dungeon Assets/DUAL Nightmare of Caiatl.png

Steps

  1. Collect and dunk Standards to begin damage setup.
  2. When Caiatl moves to a bell, kill its Bellkeepers and stun her at that bell.
  3. Damage only after a successful bell stun, then rotate to the next bell.
Fireteam Tip

Call the mechanic before moving. A clean callout usually saves more time than rushing the next step.

Team Roles

Bell Caller

Controls when bells are shot and confirms everyone is safe.

Standard Runner

Collects correct symbols in Nightmare.

Bellkeeper Clear

Deletes Bellkeepers quickly before damage or return travel.
