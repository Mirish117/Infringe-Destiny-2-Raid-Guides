Flooded Inspection Guide
Flooded Guide Pattern Guide

  1. Use the inspection image to identify the correct symbols or objects.

02
Truth / Lie
Puzzle Mechanic
Truth / Lie Guide

The Truth / Lie mechanic is used to determine which symbols, objects, or statements should be trusted and which ones should be ignored.

  1. Read all visible clues before choosing a symbol or route.
  2. Separate the truthful clue from the false clue.
  3. Use the guide image to confirm the correct interpretation.
  4. Apply the answer to the active puzzle or encounter mechanic.
Tips & Notes
  • Do not act on the first clue until the false clue has been ruled out.
  • Repeat the answer back to the team before anyone deposits, shoots, or activates anything.
03
Lockout
Encounter Mechanic
Lockout Guide

The Lockout mechanic is about preventing incorrect activations while keeping track of which options are still available to the fireteam.

  1. Identify which symbols or nodes are locked out.
  2. Call which options are still valid.
  3. Have one player confirm before the team commits to an activation.
  4. Reset the callout if a mechanic changes mid-round.
Tips & Notes
  • Use short calls like “locked,” “open,” “safe,” and “wrong” to keep comms clean.
  • If two players disagree, stop and re-check instead of guessing.
04
Isolate Preservation
Encounter Mechanic
Isolate Preservation Guide

Use this guide section to handle the Isolate Preservation mechanic, where the team must identify what should be separated, protected, preserved, or avoided.

  1. Read the mechanic state and identify the target object, symbol, or clue.
  2. Determine what needs to be isolated or preserved.
  3. Assign one player to handle the mechanic while the rest control enemies.
  4. Confirm completion before moving into the next step.
Tips & Notes
  • Keep one player dedicated to mechanic calls so the team does not talk over each other.
  • Mechanic accuracy matters more than speed during the first few runs.
05
Final Encounter
Boss / Completion

The final section combines the dungeon’s symbol reading, puzzle confirmation, and high-pressure execution. Use the reference images above whenever the team needs to confirm a mechanic before committing.

Recommended Loadout
  • Reliable add-clear weapon for fast enemy control.
  • High-burst heavy weapon for boss damage windows.
  • Survivability tools such as Well of Radiance, Ward of Dawn, healing rifts, barricades, or invisibility.
  1. Clear enemies and stabilize the arena.
  2. Read the active symbols or puzzle state.
  3. Use the correct reference image to confirm the answer.
  4. Complete the mechanic and open the damage or completion window.
  5. Repeat until the encounter is cleared.
Tips & Notes
  • Before each major action, repeat the callout once so the whole team is synced.
  • Keep the symbols reference open if your team is still learning the dungeon.
  • Clean communication is the most important part of puzzle-heavy encounters.
