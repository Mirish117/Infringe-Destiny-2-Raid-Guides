Who We Are

Born in the Dark.
Forged in the Void.

Infringe is a Destiny 2 clan built for Guardians who want to push beyond the edge — whether that's your first raid clear or your thousandth flawless run. We don't gatekeep. We sherpa. We grind. We die to the same mechanics seventeen times and laugh about it.

We run all endgame content — raids, dungeons, GMs, Trials — and we do it as a team. New Light or seasoned veteran, if you want to get better and have a crew to do it with, you found your clan.
